Good Eats

Is an app created for simple and easy recipe's. Each recipe has a brief description, directions, a list of tips, and nutritional information. With this cooking app, you'll be inspired and organized in the kitchen and on the go.

Features

The detailed search allows you to filter your recipes based on dietary and allergy information, season, dish type, and more. There's also an option to make a grocery cart in the app based on recipes you'd like to create. Videos and tutorials will be provied as well. Other than that you can also share your own recipe's on the app.

Aside from all of that, Goodeats comes with all the usual features, like the ability to create shopping lists, save your favorite recipes, and filter your search results by diet, cooking time, and foods you don't like.
